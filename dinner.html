🌙 Dinner Recipes

Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream
  • Garam masala
  • Ginger-garlic paste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan.
  3. Prepare gravy using butter and tomato puree.
  4. Add cream and cooked chicken.
  5. Simmer and serve hot.

Roti Sabzi

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare dough and make rotis.
  2. Cook rotis on tawa.
  3. Cook vegetables with spices.
  4. Serve hot roti with sabzi.

Chicken 65

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Chili powder
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Corn flour
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Add corn flour and mix.
  3. Deep fry until crispy.
  4. Serve hot.
