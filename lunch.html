South Indian Thali

Key Ingredients (for 4 servings)

>Rice, toor dal, moong dal, mixed veggies (carrot, beans, drumstick) >Sambar powder, rasam powder, tamarind, coconut, curry leaves, ghee >Yogurt, pickle, banana, papad, lemon rice extras: cashews, raisins

Steps to Cook

>Pressure cook rice & dal separately (rice:dal:turmeric ratio 1:1:pinch); make sambar with tamarind, veggies, toor dal, sambar powder.[web:37] >Prepare rasam: Boil tamarind water with tomatoes, rasam powder, temper with mustard, cumin, curry leaves; cook lemon rice with peanuts.[web:40] >Make kosambari salad (moong dal, cucumber, coconut), fry papad; arrange on banana leaf/plate with curd, pickle, banana & ghee rice.[web:37]

Chicken Biryani

Key Ingredients (for 4-6 servings)

>1.5 cups basmati rice, ½ kg chicken, yogurt (⅔ cup), ginger-garlic paste >Onions (fried), mint/coriander leaves, garam masala, saffron milk, ghee/oil >Spices: chili powder, turmeric, green chilies, lemon juice, salt

Steps to Cook

>Marinate chicken 1-2 hrs in yogurt, ginger-garlic, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, mint, coriander, salt.[web:38] >Fry sliced onions till golden; parboil soaked basmati rice with spices; layer chicken, fried onions, rice, saffron milk, ghee in pot.[web:41] >Dum cook sealed pot on low heat 20-25 mins; rest 10 mins, fluff & serve with raita (yogurt, onion, cucumber).[web:38]

Dal Chawal

Key Ingredients (for 4 servings)

>1 cup toor/moong dal mix, 2 tomatoes, 1 onion, ginger, green chilies >Tempering: ghee, cumin, garlic, red chilies, asafoetida, chili powder >Turmeric, salt; for rice: basmati/sona masuri, ghee

Steps to Cook

>Pressure cook dal with onion, tomato, ginger, chilies, turmeric, salt (4-5 whistles till mushy).[web:42] >Heat ghee, temper cumin, garlic, red chilies, asafoetida, chili powder; pour over dal, simmer 2-3 mins.[web:39] >Cook rice separately (1:2 ratio), top with ghee; serve hot dal with rice, papad & pickle.[web:42]
