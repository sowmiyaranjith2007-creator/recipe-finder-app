South Indian Thali

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Toor dal
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, drumstick)
  • Tamarind
  • Sambar powder
  • Rasam powder
  • Curd, pickle, papad

Steps

  1. Cook rice and dal separately.
  2. Prepare sambar using dal, vegetables, tamarind, and sambar powder.
  3. Make rasam using tamarind water and rasam powder.
  4. Fry papad and arrange with curd and pickle.
  5. Serve everything together as a thali.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (garam masala, turmeric, chili powder)
  • Mint and coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Cook rice until 70% done.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Layer chicken and rice in a pot.
  5. Cook on low flame (dum) for 20 minutes.

Dal Chawal

Ingredients

  • Toor dal
  • Rice
  • Onion and tomato
  • Garlic
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric and chili powder
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Pressure cook dal with turmeric and salt.
  2. Prepare tempering with ghee, cumin, and garlic.
  3. Add cooked dal and simmer.
  4. Cook rice separately.
  5. Serve dal over rice with ghee.
