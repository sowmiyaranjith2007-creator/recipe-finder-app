🍳 Breakfast Recipes

Idli

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Soak rice and dal for 6 hours.
  2. Grind into smooth batter.
  3. Ferment overnight.
  4. Steam in idli mould for 10 minutes.

Dosa

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad dal
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Soak rice and dal.
  2. Grind and ferment batter.
  3. Pour on hot tawa.
  4. Spread thin and cook crispy.

Pongal

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Moong dal
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Ghee

Preparation

  1. Cook rice and dal together.
  2. Add salt.
  3. Prepare tempering with ghee and spices.
  4. Mix and serve hot.

Poori

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Preparation

  1. Make dough.
  2. Roll into small circles.
  3. Deep fry in hot oil.
  4. Serve with curry.
