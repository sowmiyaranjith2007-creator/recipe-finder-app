🍳 Breakfast Recipes

Idli

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad dal
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Soak rice and dal.
  2. Grind into batter.
  3. Ferment overnight.
  4. Steam in mould.

Dosa

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad dal
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Prepare batter.
  2. Heat pan.
  3. Spread batter.
  4. Cook crispy.

Pongal

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Moong dal
  • Ghee

Preparation

  1. Cook rice and dal.
  2. Add spices.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Serve hot.

Poori

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Oil

Preparation

  1. Make dough.
  2. Roll circles.
  3. Deep fry.
  4. Serve hot.
