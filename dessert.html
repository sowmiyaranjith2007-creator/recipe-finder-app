🍮 Dessert Recipes

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • Maida
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Prepare dough using milk powder and maida.
  2. Make small balls.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup with cardamom.
  5. Soak fried balls in syrup.

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Saffron
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Boil milk and curdle using lemon juice.
  2. Make soft paneer balls.
  3. Cook in sugar syrup.
  4. Soak in thickened milk with saffron.
  5. Serve chilled.

Payasam

Ingredients

  • Vermicelli
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cashews
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Roast vermicelli in ghee.
  2. Boil milk and add vermicelli.
  3. Add sugar and cook.
  4. Fry cashews and raisins.
  5. Mix and serve.

Mysore Pak

Ingredients

  • Gram flour (besan)
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Prepare sugar syrup.
  2. Add roasted gram flour.
  3. Pour ghee slowly while stirring.
  4. Cook until thick.
  5. Set and cut into pieces.
